The alpha is born

Tue Jan 10, 2017 by tercen

After working throughout 2016. We have a great alpha version of the Tercen Cloud. We focussed on issues which researchers face day to day when dealing with large amount of data.

We are very proud to deliver the following:

  • Interactive multi-dimensional visuals
  • Workflow editor
  • A new visual for pairwise plots called multi-pairwise
  • A wizard to help researchers perform workflows
  • A rtercen R library
  • Ability to organize researchers into Projects and Teams
  • An up-to-date cloud-base architecture using docker, CouchDB and cephFS
  • A invitation system for new users

The alpha version allows us to demo the concepts and go out to the researchers and hear what they say about the approach. This feedback is valuable and is the major input for our beta (scheduled begining of 2018).
